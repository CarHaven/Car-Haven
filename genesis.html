Genesis

History: Genesis began as a luxury model under Hyundai and officially became its own brand in 2015. Known for its premium craftsmanship and advanced tech, Genesis quickly earned global praise for design and quality.

Relations: Genesis is a sub-brand of Hyundai and shares platforms and engineering with Hyundai and Kia. Despite this, it maintains a distinct identity focused on luxury and elegance.
