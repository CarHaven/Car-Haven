Alfa Romeo

History: Alfa Romeo is an Italian luxury car manufacturer known for its stunning design and performance. With a legacy of racing and innovation, Alfa has a cult following among enthusiasts.

Relations: Alfa Romeo is part of Stellantis, sharing platforms and tech with brands like Fiat, Maserati, and Jeep.

Most Famous Car Models of Alfa Romeo:

The Alfa Romeo Giulia is a celebration of Italian engineering — a luxury sedan that delivers precision handling, powerful turbocharged engines, and a driver-focused cockpit. Its aggressive stance and bold grille make it instantly recognizable, while its refined interior ensures every ride is as thrilling as it is comfortable.

Blending the versatility of an SUV with the soul of a sports car, the Stelvio brings Alfa’s racing heritage to a family-friendly frame. Its swift acceleration, responsive steering, and upscale interior make it a rare combination of utility and excitement — perfect for enthusiasts who refuse to settle.

Pure adrenaline on four wheels, the Alfa Romeo 4C is a mid-engine marvel built for the track but just street-legal enough for everyday thrills. Its carbon fiber monocoque chassis keeps it light and nimble, while its sculpted body turns heads with every curve. It’s not a car — it’s an experience.
