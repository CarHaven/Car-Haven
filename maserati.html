Maserati

History: Maserati was founded in 1914 in Bologna, Italy. Known for its racing roots and luxury sports sedans, it’s always walked the line between performance and prestige—often with a devilish V8 soundtrack.

Relations: Maserati is part of Stellantis, alongside Alfa Romeo and Dodge. It has previously shared engines with Ferrari, giving it serious horsepower cred.
