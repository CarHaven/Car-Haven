Renault

History: Renault has been at the heart of European mobility since 1899. Known for innovation, it brought the world compact cars like the Clio and also dominated Formula 1 with legendary engines and teams.

Relations: Renault is the core of the Renault–Nissan–Mitsubishi Alliance. The group shares platforms, EV tech, and global strategy—powering both city streets and racetracks.

Most Famous Car Models of Renault:

Europe’s favorite hatchback. Nimble, reliable, and easy on the eyes. The Clio is Renault’s golden child.
