Peugeot

History: Peugeot began its automotive journey in the 19th century and quickly became a staple in French car culture. From practical hatchbacks to the legendary 205 GTi, it balanced economy with motorsport brilliance, especially in rally racing.

Relations: Peugeot is part of Stellantis, alongside brands like Citroën, Fiat, and Jeep. It shares technology, platforms, and innovation across the group.
