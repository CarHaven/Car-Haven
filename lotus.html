Lotus

History: Founded in 1952, Lotus built its name in motorsport and engineering excellence. Famous for cars like the Elise and Exige, Lotus emphasizes lightweight design and tight handling over raw power.

Relations: Lotus is now owned by Geely, which also owns Volvo. It’s shifting toward electric performance while maintaining its purist DNA.

Most Famous Car Models of Lotus:

A barebones, driver-focused icon. Lightweight to the extreme, the Elise is loved for its sharp handling and minimalistic design.

Lotus’ bold electric hypercar. It boasts nearly 2,000 horsepower and futuristic design, marking a new chapter for the brand.

The track-focused sibling of the Elise. It’s louder, meaner, and ready to rip laps around any circuit with surgical precision.
