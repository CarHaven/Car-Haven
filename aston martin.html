History: Founded in 1913, Aston Martin is a British luxury sports car brand known for its sleek styling and strong performance. It rose to iconic status through its long association with the James Bond film franchise.

Relations: Aston Martin has had partnerships over the years with brands like Ford (former parent), and more recently Mercedes-AMG for engine and tech sharing.
