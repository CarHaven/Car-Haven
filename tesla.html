Tesla

History: Founded in 2003, Tesla disrupted the auto industry with sleek EVs, insane acceleration, and a minimalist vibe. The Model S, 3, X, and Y reshaped how people see electric cars.

Relations: Tesla remains independent, unlike traditional manufacturers. However, it collaborates on battery tech with Panasonic and has inspired global EV trends, pushing legacy automakers to go electric.

Most Famous Car Models of Tesla:

The Model S redefined performance sedans with blistering speed, long range, and a minimalist interior. It’s the luxury EV that made Tesla a global name.

Affordable, efficient, and futuristic—the Model 3 brought electric cars to the masses. It’s one of the best-selling EVs worldwide.

The Cybertruck is Tesla’s angular, stainless steel rebellion. It’s a futuristic pickup that looks like it rolled out of a sci-fi movie—and into endless memes.
