Lexus

History: Lexus launched in 1989 as Toyota’s luxury division, aimed at the U.S. market. Known for its craftsmanship, reliability, and whisper-quiet rides, it quickly became a top luxury contender.

Relations: Lexus is owned by Toyota and shares many platforms and hybrid systems. It often pioneers new tech that later appears in Toyota models.

Most Famous Car Models of Lexus:

A legend in the making. The V10-powered LFA was a precision-built supercar with a howl that haunts gearheads. Limited, exotic, and unforgettable.

The luxury SUV that started it all. The RX was one of the first crossovers to blend high-end features with family practicality. Smooth, refined, and always popular.

A stunning grand tourer with jaw-dropping looks and a throaty V8. It proves Lexus isn’t afraid to break from boring with true performance luxury.
