Hyundai

History: Hyundai was founded in 1967 and quickly became one of the largest car manufacturers in the world. Known for affordable, high-quality vehicles and innovation, it has transformed into a serious global competitor.

Relations: Hyundai is the parent company of Genesis and a major stakeholder in Kia. The three brands often share platforms and technology while targeting different market segments.
