History: Ferrari was founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 and became a symbol of speed, luxury, and elite performance. Its legacy in Formula 1 and iconic road cars like the LaFerrari and F40 made it a global legend.

Relations: Ferrari was once part of Fiat but later spun off into its own independent brand. It maintains ties with Stellantis through past shared history, but now focuses purely on exclusive performance cars.
