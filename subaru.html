Subaru

History: Subaru, founded in 1953, made its mark with durable AWD vehicles and the iconic boxer engine. From the rally-bred WRX to the practical Outback, Subaru balances thrill with reliability.

Relations: Subaru has partnered with Toyota for tech and development—most notably with the BRZ/86 sports car duo—sharing platforms and hybrid advancements.

Most Famous Car Models of Subaru:

The Impreza WRX is a rally legend. With turbocharged power, all-wheel drive, and aggressive styling, it’s earned a spot in motorsport history—and in every car enthusiast’s heart.

A pioneer in the crossover segment, the Outback mixes SUV toughness with car comfort. Beloved by adventurers and families alike, it’s the go-anywhere wagon that refuses to quit.

Developed with Toyota, the BRZ is a pure driver’s car—rear-wheel drive, lightweight, and responsive. It brings old-school driving joy in a modern coupe body.
