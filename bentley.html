History: Bentley Motors Limited is a British manufacturer of luxury cars and SUVs founded in 1919. Known for hand-crafted interiors and powerful engines, Bentley represents elegance, prestige, and performance.

Relations: Bentley is a part of the Volkswagen Group and shares platforms and technologies with Audi and Porsche, especially under the hood and in infotainment systems.
