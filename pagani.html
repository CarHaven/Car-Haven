Pagani

[ Image Placeholder ]

History: Founded in 1992 by Horacio Pagani, the brand is known for crafting hypercars that are both engineering marvels and artistic masterpieces. Its debut model, the Zonda, redefined what it meant to blend speed with luxury. Pagani is rare, exclusive, and unapologetically extra.

Relations: Pagani has collaborated with Mercedes-AMG for engines, notably the V12s powering its models. While independent, its deep ties with AMG provide its cars both brute force and refinement.
