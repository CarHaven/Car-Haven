Buick

History: Buick is one of the oldest American car brands, founded in 1899. It helped launch General Motors and became a symbol of quiet luxury and refined performance. Known for being a step up from Chevrolet in style and comfort.

Relations: Buick is a core brand under General Motors (GM), sharing platforms with Chevrolet and GMC. It plays a major role in GM’s global strategy, especially in China.

Most Famous Car Models of Buick:

The Enclave is Buick’s flagship SUV, known for its spacious interior, smooth ride, and premium touches. It’s a refined choice for families who want class without shouting it.

A full-size sedan that offers quiet elegance, the LaCrosse emphasizes comfort, technology, and a whisper-quiet cabin. It's a fan favorite among luxury cruisers.

The Encore is Buick’s compact SUV—a nimble, city-friendly ride with upscale vibes. It’s stylish, efficient, and has that just-right premium touch for urban explorers.
