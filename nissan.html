Nissan

History: Founded in 1933, Nissan is a titan of Japanese engineering. It gave the world icons like the Skyline GT-R, the 350Z/370Z, and now the all-electric Leaf. Whether speed or sustainability, Nissan’s been in the game.

Relations: Nissan is a core part of the Renault–Nissan–Mitsubishi Alliance. It shares platforms and innovation with Renault and Mitsubishi, especially in EV development and global markets.

Most Famous Car Models of Nissan:

The Godzilla. A twin-turbo V6 monster with insane acceleration and all-wheel drive precision. A supercar killer in disguise.

Lightweight, rear-wheel drive, and manual—pure driving pleasure. A Z-legacy staple.

One of the world’s best-selling electric cars. Efficient, silent, and ready for the future.
