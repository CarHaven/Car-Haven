Suzuki

History: Suzuki began as a loom company before rolling out motorcycles and compact cars in post-war Japan. Known for its efficiency and reliability, it’s huge in Asia with cars like the Swift and Alto.

Relations: Suzuki has partnered with Toyota in recent years, sharing hybrid tech and co-developing models, especially for markets like India where Suzuki dominates.

Most Famous Car Models of Suzuki:

The Swift is Suzuki’s global superstar. A peppy hatchback with sporty looks and excellent mileage, it’s loved for city driving and affordability without feeling cheap.

Small, reliable, and insanely popular, the Alto is the everyman’s car in Asia. It’s compact yet durable and one of the most sold nameplates in India.

A tiny off-roader with cult status, the Jimny punches above its weight with true 4x4 capability and retro charm. It’s a rugged icon in a mini package.
