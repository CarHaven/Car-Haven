Jaguar

History: Jaguar began in the 1920s in the UK, building stylish luxury and performance vehicles. It's known for blending British refinement with racing pedigree and innovation.

Relations: Jaguar is owned by Tata Motors and shares platforms with Land Rover. Under the Jaguar Land Rover (JLR) group, the brands collaborate on tech and design while maintaining their unique vibes.

A sexy coupe or convertible—pure performance and attitude. British muscle in a tuxedo.
