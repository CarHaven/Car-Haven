Lamborghini

History: Founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini, this Italian legend started as a rebellion against Ferrari. Known for outrageous designs and V12 madness, Lamborghini is the poster child of supercar extravagance.

Relations: Lamborghini is owned by the Volkswagen Group under Audi. It shares platforms and technology with Audi R8, Porsche, and others, but with its own wild flair.

Most Famous Car Models of Lamborghini:

The Aventador is an icon—V12, scissor doors, and angular aggression. It’s pure Lambo chaos with a spaceship presence and an exhaust that spits thunder.
