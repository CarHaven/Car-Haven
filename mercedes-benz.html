Mercedes-Benz

History: One of the oldest automakers, Mercedes-Benz traces its roots back to Karl Benz’s first gasoline-powered car in 1886. It’s been a symbol of luxury, innovation, and precision ever since.

Relations: Mercedes-Benz is part of the Mercedes-Benz Group and has collaborated with AMG (its performance wing), McLaren (briefly), and smart. It also owns Maybach for ultra-luxury models.

Most Famous Car Models of Mercedes-Benz:

The luxury flagship. Tech-packed, ultra-smooth ride. Often the first to feature innovations before anyone else dares.

Brute elegance. Performance coupe with AMG's heart-thumping V8s. Track beast wrapped in class.

The G-Wagen. Military roots, celebrity icon. Boxy, bold, and insanely capable off-road—or just flexing in traffic.
