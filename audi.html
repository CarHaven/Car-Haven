Audi

History: Audi is a German automaker founded in 1909, known for its advanced technology, clean design, and the iconic Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It’s recognized for blending performance with sophistication.

Relations: Audi is part of the Volkswagen Group and shares platforms, engines, and technologies with Porsche, Lamborghini, Bentley, and VW itself.

Most Famous Car Models of Audi:

The R8 is Audi’s halo car, powered by a roaring V10 and built with a lightweight chassis and Quattro grip. It’s as much a track monster as it is a head-turning work of art.

The Audi A4 is a compact executive sedan with clean styling, a luxurious cabin, and refined turbocharged powertrains. A fan favorite for blending comfort and sport.

Audi’s best-selling SUV that combines practicality, luxury, and tech. The Q5 is versatile with all-wheel-drive standard and interior luxury that punches above its class.
