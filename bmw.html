BMW

History: BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke) was founded in 1916 in Germany and began with aircraft engines before evolving into one of the world’s top luxury and performance automakers. It’s known for precision, sportiness, and design.

Relations: BMW owns Mini and Rolls-Royce. Its platforms and engines are shared across these brands while keeping their identities distinct.

Most Famous Car Models of BMW:

The M3 is the holy grail of sport sedans. A perfect blend of daily comfort and savage performance with a roaring inline-6 engine and razor-sharp handling.

The i8 was BMW’s futuristic hybrid dream. With its scissor doors, spaceship silhouette, and eco-meets-speed engineering, it turned heads wherever it rolled.

The X5 is BMW’s luxury midsize SUV. Plush yet powerful, it's a family hauler that knows how to fly down the autobahn in style.
