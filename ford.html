Ford

History: Founded by Henry Ford in 1903, Ford revolutionized the auto industry with the Model T and assembly line production. It remains one of the most influential and enduring carmakers globally.

Relations: Ford owns Lincoln and has previously held stakes in Volvo, Aston Martin, and Mazda. Though independent now, it has historically shared tech and platforms with these brands.
