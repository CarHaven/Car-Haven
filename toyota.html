Toyota

History: Toyota, founded in 1937, rose to dominance with a focus on quality, efficiency, and durability. Known for icons like the Corolla, Supra, and Prius, Toyota blends reliability with subtle performance.

Relations: Toyota owns Lexus and has partnerships with Subaru, Suzuki, and Mazda. Its joint ventures help expand hybrid and EV tech across brands globally.

Most Famous Car Models of Toyota:

With over 50 million sold, the Corolla is the world’s best-selling car. Reliable, fuel-efficient, and affordable—it’s the definition of dependable daily driving.

The Supra is Toyota’s performance legend, reborn in collaboration with BMW. Loved for its turbocharged punch and rear-wheel drive thrills.

The Prius brought hybrids to the mainstream. It’s not flashy, but it’s iconic for efficiency and a symbol of eco-conscious driving since the early 2000s.
