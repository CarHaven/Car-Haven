Porsche

History: Founded in 1931, Porsche is a German icon famous for the 911 and a deep motorsport legacy. It balances luxury, speed, and engineering mastery in every vehicle—whether it’s the track-ready GT3 or the electric Taycan.

Relations: Porsche is part of the Volkswagen Group. It shares technology with Audi, Bentley, and Lamborghini—while still maintaining a unique design philosophy centered on performance and precision.
