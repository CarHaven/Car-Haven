Mazda

History: Founded in 1920, Mazda started as a cork company before transitioning to automobiles. It gained fame for its use of rotary engines and its dedication to driving dynamics, especially in models like the MX-5 Miata.

Relations: Mazda previously partnered with Ford and now collaborates with Toyota on future EVs. It's one of the few brands to consistently embrace fun, lightweight vehicles.

Most Famous Car Models of Mazda:

The lightweight roadster that made open-top driving cool again. Rear-wheel drive, perfect balance, and a big smile factor. A cult icon.

The rotary-powered legend. Sleek, stylish, and an absolute drift darling. A JDM hero that still lives rent-free in every tuner’s heart.

A modern SUV that nails premium vibes without a luxury badge. It’s the crossover that proves practical can still mean stylish and fun.
