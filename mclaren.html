McLaren

History: Founded in 1963 by Bruce McLaren, this British icon is deeply rooted in Formula One. It transitioned into road cars with the legendary McLaren F1 and now produces cutting-edge supercars like the 720S and Artura.

Relations: McLaren Automotive operates independently from its F1 team. It shares racing DNA across divisions but doesn’t rely on larger manufacturers for platforms or engines.

Most Famous Car Models of McLaren:

The icon. Naturally aspirated V12. Gold-lined engine bay. Center seating. Still one of the fastest and most expensive cars ever made.

Aero beast. Twin-turbo V8. Sleek butterfly doors. It redefined the modern supercar game with its insane speed and drivability.

Hybrid hypercar royalty. Part of the ‘Holy Trinity’ with LaFerrari and 918. Brutal power meets tech wizardry—pure madness.
