Rolls-Royce

History: Rolls-Royce was founded in 1906, born from the pursuit of “the best car in the world.” The brand redefined luxury with meticulous craftsmanship and silent power. From the Phantom to the Ghost, every model is a rolling palace.

Relations: Now owned by BMW, Rolls-Royce benefits from German precision while keeping its British soul intact. It shares tech with BMW’s top-tier models but with a style all its own.

Most Famous Car Models of Rolls-Royce:

The Rolls-Royce Phantom is the ultimate symbol of luxury. It floats on roads like a dream and boasts a handcrafted interior that feels like a private jet. Unmatched in elegance, it’s reserved for the few who demand the very best.

The Ghost offers a more “subtle” take on Rolls-Royce refinement. Understated yet powerful, it's designed for those who prefer to drive themselves while still basking in the brand’s signature quiet sophistication.

Rolls-Royce’s first SUV doesn’t just follow the crowd—it redefines it. The Cullinan blends off-road capability with extreme comfort, wrapping it all in unmistakable Rolls-Royce luxury. A beast in a velvet robe.
