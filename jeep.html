Jeep

History: Born during WWII, Jeep became a symbol of freedom and durability. It pioneered the SUV segment and remains one of the most iconic off-road brands in the world.

Relations: Jeep is part of Stellantis and shares components with Dodge, Chrysler, and Fiat. Its rugged DNA still stands out across shared platforms.
